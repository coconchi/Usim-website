About USim

Our mission

We are a research team that forms part of the National Laboratory for Public Policy (LNPP) in Mexico City. We specialize in the development and use of computational models for the analysis of social phenomenon and the effects of public policy and its implementation.

Our techniques

The USim develops and implements computational models that simulate scenarios that approximate reality. Though the specific method of simulation varies between projects, the unit is particularly specialized in agent-based models.

Our models cover a wide range of topics related to the social sciences and public policy needs in Mexico. At the moment we are developing models related to healthcare reform, fiscal reform, traffic and movility, and public policy networks. We partner closely with other research organizations, government bodies and the private sector as well as other divisions of the LNPP.
